The checklist

Go through each section. Check off what you already have in place.

Be honest. The gaps you find are exactly where you're losing time and money every week. At the end, tally your score to see where your firm stands.

Client Intake & Onboarding
9 checkpoints
  • Standardized intake form captures all required client information in one place
  • Intake form automatically creates a CRM record — no manual data entry
  • New client trigger automatically sends a welcome email sequence
  • Contract is generated and sent automatically on client sign-up
  • E-signature is embedded in the contract flow — no printing or scanning
  • First invoice is generated and sent automatically on contract signing
  • Document request list is sent automatically — not manually compiled each time
  • Internal tasks are assigned automatically when a new client is added
  • Client portal or shared folder is set up automatically on sign-up
Billing & Invoicing
9 checkpoints
  • Recurring invoices are generated and sent automatically on schedule
  • Payment reminders are sent automatically at set intervals — not manually
  • Overdue invoices trigger an escalation sequence without manual intervention
  • Invoice data syncs automatically to QuickBooks or Xero — no double entry
  • Payment received triggers an automatic receipt and CRM update
  • You can see outstanding balances across all clients in one view — not a spreadsheet dig
  • ACH or card payments are accepted online — clients don't need to call or mail cheques
  • Revenue by client and service is visible without manual reporting
  • Billing errors or mismatches are flagged automatically — not found manually
Document Management
8 checkpoints
  • Client documents are stored in a consistent, searchable folder structure — not scattered across inboxes
  • Document requests are tracked — you know at a glance what's been received and what's outstanding
  • Follow-up reminders for missing documents are sent automatically
  • Completed documents trigger the next step in the workflow automatically
  • Documents are named and filed consistently — no manual renaming or sorting
  • Tax deadline reminders are sent to clients automatically
  • Signed documents are stored and linked to the client record automatically
  • Sensitive documents are shared securely — not via unencrypted email
Internal Operations
9 checkpoints
  • New client tasks are automatically assigned to the right team member — not delegated in a meeting
  • Recurring client work is automatically scheduled — not added manually each cycle
  • Team knows exactly what's outstanding without a status meeting
  • Capacity is visible — you know who's overloaded before it becomes a problem
  • Client status updates don't require chasing teammates for information
  • Handoffs between team members are documented and tracked — nothing falls through the cracks
  • SOPs (standard operating procedures) exist for your top 5 most repeated processes
  • Onboarding a new team member doesn't depend entirely on you being present
  • Your firm can operate for a full day without you in it
Reporting & Visibility
7 checkpoints
  • Monthly revenue is visible in real time — not calculated manually at month end
  • You know which clients are most profitable without running a custom report
  • At-risk clients (overdue, unresponsive, nearing renewal) are flagged automatically
  • Client renewal and anniversary dates trigger automated reminders
  • Time saved by automation is tracked — you can quantify the ROI
  • Monthly performance snapshot is generated automatically — not compiled by hand
  • Data from your tools (QBO, CRM, billing) is consolidated in one place
Client Communication & Retention
8 checkpoints
  • New clients receive an automated welcome sequence — not a one-off email you write manually
  • Clients receive proactive updates on their work — not just when they ask
  • Upsell or service expansion opportunities are flagged automatically based on client data
  • Client satisfaction is checked at regular intervals — not only when something goes wrong
  • Long-term clients receive anniversary or milestone touchpoints automatically
  • Inbound enquiries are responded to within 1 hour — even outside business hours
  • FAQ responses are automated — your team isn't answering the same 10 questions repeatedly
  • After-hours calls are captured and followed up — not missed and forgotten
