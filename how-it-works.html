Week 1
Most firms try to automate broken processes — and wonder why nothing improves.
Before we touch a single tool, we map exactly how your firm operates today. We identify where time is being lost, where errors are being introduced, and where the real bottlenecks live. This is also where we find the billing leaks and workflow gaps quietly cutting into your margins. Foundation is what separates firms that try automation and fail from firms that implement it and win.

→

Layering
"Your client signed. Now stop doing everything manually."
Week 2
The moment a client says yes should trigger a system — not a to-do list.
We build the automated sequence that runs from signed contract to active client. Contracts go out automatically. Invoices are generated on trigger. Welcome emails and document requests fire without anyone touching them. Your team's first real interaction with a new client is the work — not the admin. Clients get a professional, seamless experience from day one — which directly improves satisfaction and retention.
What this fixes
→
Manually sending contracts, invoices, and welcome emails
→
Chasing clients for documents and information
→
Internal tasks falling through the cracks
→
The first 30 days feeling disorganized on both sides

Contract automation Invoice auto-generation Welcome email sequence Document request workflow Task assignment automation

Revenue leaks happen quietly — missed follow-ups, late invoices, inconsistent tracking.

You stop leaving money on the table — not because you're more disciplined, but because the system is. For most firms, this phase alone recovers enough revenue to justify the entire implementation.

Billing bottlenecks that slow cash flow

Invoices that go out late or get forgotten entirely

→

Recurring billing automation Payment tracking system Smart follow-up sequences Revenue visibility dashboard Overdue escalation workflow
