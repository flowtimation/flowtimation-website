About Flowtimation

Built by someone who's lived inside the workflows we fix.

Flowtimation was founded on a simple observation: accounting firms aren't struggling because they have too much work — they're struggling because of how that work moves through their business. After years working inside accounting and finance operations, building automation workflows, integrating disconnected systems, and watching firsthand how much time and revenue firms lose every day to manual processes that should have been automated long ago, the pattern became impossible to ignore. Staff burning out on repetitive tasks. Clients leaving because the experience felt inconsistent. Margins eroding because billing was an afterthought. Owners too deep in the weeds to actually lead. The tools exist. The technology is there. What most firms are missing is the time, the expertise, and a structured approach to putting it all together — and that's the gap Flowtimation was built to fill.

"The problem is not workload. It is workflow. Once a firm solves that — everything changes."

Our approach in practice
01
We eliminate before we automate
Every engagement starts with a workflow audit. We remove what doesn't need to exist before we touch a single automation tool. You can't systematize chaos — so we clear it first.
02
We work in your existing stack
We don't ask you to switch platforms. We connect and automate what you already use — QuickBooks, Microsoft 365, Outlook, Xero, Stripe — and make them work together.
03
We minimize your involvement
You have a firm to run. Your involvement during implementation is focused and purposeful — an onboarding call, weekly check-ins, and a sign-off at go-live. We handle the rest.
04
We hand over, not hand off
Every implementation ends with full documentation, team training, and a working system your team understands. You're not dependent on us to operate it — we're there to keep it improving.
